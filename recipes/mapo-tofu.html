Mapo Tofu Recipe

Description:

Mapo tofu is one of my favorite dishes. My bf makes really good mapo tofu and you should eat it too.

Ingredients:

  • soft to medium tofu
  • ground meat (optional)
  • chicken broth
  • broad bean paste
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • ground seasoning:
    • Cardemom
    • Clove
    • Coriander
    • Cumin
      •

Steps:

  1. Grind up your seasonings
  2. heat up oil in a wok and cook the meat
  3. add ginger, garlic, and seasonings to wok and cook until well incorporated
  4. add the rest of ingredients
  5. eat it yummy yummy
