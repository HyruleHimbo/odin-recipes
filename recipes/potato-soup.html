Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • potato
  • onion
  • leek
  • chive
  • chicken broth
  • heavy cream
  • cheddar cheese
  • bacon
  1. cook onions and leeks with oil in a pot until softened
  2. add potato and chicken broth and bring to a boil
  3. boil until potatoes are cooked and soft
  4. blend the mixture until smoothed to preference
  5. add heavy cream to desired creaminess
  6. garnish with chopped chive, crushed bacon, and shredded cheddar
  7. add whatever seasoning to taste
